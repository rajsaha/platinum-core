Centralised BBU System

The concept is typically a network with Centralised BBUs co-located in centralised site away from antennas. The appeal (benefits) for user:

  • Better usage experience with improved 4G LTE coverage
  • Good coverage reception
  • No more big tower with high power transmitting antenna

We're here to introduce new technology to enhance shared cellular services by using fiber optics that can lead with no microwave transmitters. And also to reduce the duplication of towers amongst the providers. A smaller coverage area translate to lower heights for transmission and less power radiated.

Small Cell

We will be using a sleek 22 meter multifunctional poles with only 1m x 1m space consumption

Fiber Optics

Pure fiber optics network development. No microwave transmitters.

Safety & Security

Provide with 3 CCTV that are linked to local authority. Include free WiFi to the residents of 100 meters away and also LED lights to the residence for security

Being an independent, carrier neutral infrastructure provider, PCS ensures all the cellular provider can share a common facility without having to set up their own. Our objective is to reduce the duplication of structures amongst providers.
